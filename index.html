About Maulana Bashir Farooq Qadri

Maulana Bashir Farooqi Qadri is the Chairman of Saylani Welfare Trust, which is one of the biggest welfare organizations in Pakistan. In recent years Saylani Welfare Trust has steadily grown into one of the most active and renowned welfare organizations in the country but many people are unaware from the life and profile of the Maulana Bashir who established the organization to help the people of Pakistan. Bashir Ahmed Polani was born on 3rd March 1963 in the month of Ramazan in the city of Shikarpur, Sindh. He belongs to a noble and religious family from the Bantawa Community and his father Abdul Ghaffar Polani was a successful businessmen. He completed his schooling from Islamia High School Shikarpur and then travelled to Karachi in 1975 to pursue college & Islamic studies. In Karachi he got admission in Abdullah Haroon College from where he completed his intermediate education. After completing college he pursued his Islamic studies from Darul-ul-Uloom Amjadia from where he successfully did his Dars-e-Nizami. In early 1990’s Maulana Bashir Farooqi along with some other people started their charitable and social work. Later on 15 May 1999, he established a non-profit organization which was named ‘Saylani Welfare International Trust’. From the establishment of Saylani Welfare till today he is serving as the Chairman, leading all the projects and work of the organization. What makes him more unique is the fact that he works voluntarily for the organization.
